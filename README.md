# SingleKey-Code-Catcher
A FireFox addon for Extended Bosch thermostat ha-pro (CT200 cloud) integrations in HomeAssistant. This addon will help you extract the needed key for your installation.

This is the HA Integration: https://github.com/CaseyRo/ha_bosch

Follow the instructions in HA. It tells you to login to your SingleKey ID. After login it will not redirect the site, a "Continue" will pop-up. Press on that and then check on this addon, you will now have the needed key to continue your setup/re-login.

Good luck! :)
